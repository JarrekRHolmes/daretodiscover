Q: Hey, I’m supposed to get some immunizations for the school, where am I supposed to do that?

A: You can go to UHS at 1060 Morewood Avenue, but check in your booklet for the exact times that they are doing immunizations.


Q: I need to get allergy shots but I don’t have a car to get me back and forth to my appointments on time. Is there anywhere closer that I can get them done?

A: You can arrange to have your allergy shots done at UHS at specific times, although this will have to be arranged with UHS and your healthcare provider.
